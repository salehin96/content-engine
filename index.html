What the system demonstrates

  • Systems design. A seven-phase pipeline where every step reads specific files and writes specific files, so the whole thing is auditable end to end.
  • Multi-agent orchestration. Research, writing, and adversarial review run as separate specialist agents, with a cold handoff so quality is judged independently of who wrote it.
  • Machine-enforced quality. Output cannot ship unless it proves it followed the rules, on top of a deterministic validator for style, structure, and linking.
  • Self-healing lifecycle. The live CMS and sitemap continuously reconcile the plan, so tracked state can never quietly drift from reality.
